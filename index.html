Fencing - an Ancient Sport for the Exciting Modern World

Discover how Zorro, The Three Musketeers, Link and Jedi Knights have stayed fit throughout the centuries!

Fencing Academy of Calgary invites you to start the sword play by joining our club! Set a new personal goal to improve yourself physically. Get rid of a few pounds easily and with fun! You can even join some of our club members in winning medals at Provincial, Canadian and even international championships! Or you may want your kid to excel in this individual sport with future benefits such as getting scholarships at the world's top universities.

Our fencing club is the right place for you or your child. Gold medal winners at Provincial, Western and Canadian National Championships have been coached by our world-class fencing master.

Choose your weapon and let the fun begin!
